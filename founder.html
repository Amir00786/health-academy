Meet the founder

Dr. Musab Habib

Founder, iHealth Academy

Dr. Musab Habib is a physician, radiology resident, medical insurance professional, and healthcare educator committed to making high-quality medical education more practical, accessible, and future-ready.

With experience spanning diagnostic radiology, healthcare insurance, clinical decision-making, and healthcare quality, he founded iHealth Academy with one simple belief:

"Learning should not only help healthcare professionals pass examinations — it should help them make better decisions, provide safer patient care, and build meaningful careers."

At iHealth Academy, every course is designed to bridge the gap between theory and real-world practice, empowering healthcare professionals with knowledge they can apply confidently from day one.

As artificial intelligence reshapes healthcare, our commitment remains unchanged: combining human expertise with innovative technology to create a smarter, more effective learning experience — without losing the values that define great healthcare professionals.

Our Vision

To become one of the world's most trusted digital healthcare learning platforms, empowering healthcare professionals through innovation, evidence-based education, and responsible artificial intelligence.

Our Mission

We strive to deliver practical, high-quality healthcare education that enables professionals to learn efficiently, think critically, and excel in their careers. By integrating expert knowledge, modern technology, and AI-powered learning, we aim to make continuous professional development more accessible, engaging, and impactful for healthcare professionals worldwide.
