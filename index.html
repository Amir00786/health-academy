Who we are

A career deserves a companion,
not a course catalog.

For twenty years, healthcare education treated a career like a single test — pass the exam, get the license, you're done. That was never quite true, and it's even less true now. A radiologist choosing a subspecialty, a nurse preparing for NP boards, an intern realizing pre-authorization paperwork will shape half her working week — these are different problems that show up at different moments, and most platforms are only ever built to solve one of them. iHealth Academy exists to meet more of them, in the order you actually run into them.

Built for real stages

Not a syllabus. Content built around actual moments — choosing a specialty, prepping for boards, learning the insurance systems nobody explains in school.

AI-era from day one

The next thirty years of medicine will be shaped by AI as much as by anatomy. We build every track with that already assumed, not bolted on later.

Made by people who've been there

Every track starts with someone who actually worked the job — not a curriculum written at a distance from the work it's meant to prepare you for.
