PRE-AUTH SPECIALIST · PRACTICAL EXAM

Twenty cases. Fifteen decisions. One certificate.

This simulates a real pre-authorization queue. Review each case's history, health questionnaire, and documents — ask up to two questions if you need more — then decide. Score more than 7 out of 15 and you pass.

  • The dashboard shows a full queue like a real hospital line — cases are color-coded by how long they've been waiting.
  • Fifteen cases are real and count toward your score. A few tiles are marked "not yet checked in" — they're just there for realism.
  • Each case gives you a patient summary, insured claims history, HQ status, and three documents — not all of them matter.
  • You may ask up to two questions per case for more detail. Choose them wisely.
  • Decide: Approve, Partially Approve, Reject, or Refer for Review — the same categories used in real pre-auth software.
  • You'll see your full results — and the correct reasoning for every case — only at the end.
